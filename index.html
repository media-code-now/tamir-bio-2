About Me About

Tamir Gluska leads by building — from the ground up.

Tamir Gluska is a multidisciplinary entrepreneur and the founder of Gluska Management, a brand management and investment firm overseeing a growing portfolio of ventures across wellness, lifestyle retail, and real estate development.

    Gluska Management was created to centralize and scale Tamir’s business ventures by providing strategic leadership, operational infrastructure, and brand oversight. Under its umbrella are:
  • Green Planet — a CBD company offering premium, high-potency wellness products with locations in Las Vegas, San Francisco, and Hawaii.
  • BloomStem — a luxury wellness brand built for the modern self-care consumer.
  • Seven Builders — a design-forward construction company dedicated to quality, trust, and smart development.

Tamir’s entrepreneurial journey began with a personal mission: to find better wellness solutions. That journey led to the creation of Green Planet, which grew from a single Las Vegas storefront into a nationally recognized CBD brand. As the pandemic reshaped the retail landscape, Tamir quickly pivoted to eCommerce and leaned into expansion, growing both his customer base and brand footprint.

In 2023, Tamir launched Seven Builders to bring his operational and branding expertise into the construction space, merging disciplined leadership with a commitment to excellence. Through BloomStem, he continues to explore the intersection of wellness, beauty, and brand storytelling.

At the center of it all is Gluska Management, the structure that allows Tamir to do what he does best: build, scale, and lead with purpose.

"From wellness to construction, I build companies that improve lives."
Tamir Gluska
